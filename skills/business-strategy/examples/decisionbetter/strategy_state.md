## Implementation Blueprint: Option A — Productized Workshop Ladder (Updated)

**30-Day Sprint (Stealth-Compatible)**
- Week 1:
  - Draft 5 product names, scope descriptions, and outcome statements (outcome-led: "3 workflows implemented" not "half-day workshop")
  - Register Anthropic Claude Partner Network (30 minutes) — PRIORITY
  - Activate warm network: 10 personal conversations with relevant contacts (Option N)
  - Design diagnostic intake form (5-10 questions, online — Google Forms or Typeform)
- Week 2-3:
  - Update website: add product pages with prices, intake form link, and 15-min booking link (Calendly/cal.com)
  - Write the outcome guarantee specifics for each tier (see below)
  - Begin direct outreach campaign: 10 personalised messages/day (Option O)
  - Brief 5 trusted contacts on new pricing for informal referral conversations
- Week 4:
  - Attempt to close first 3 workshop bookings (Taster or Sprint tier)
  - Approach 3 potential referral partners (accountants, business coaches)
  - Pitch to speak at 1-2 private member events (Chamber, CPA Australia chapter)
  - First SEO blog post: "How [Industry] businesses are using AI in 2026 — what actually works"

**Outcome Guarantee Specifications by Tier:**
- **AI Quick Wins Session ($997)**: You leave with at least 3 specific, named AI workflow opportunities for your business, or we refund the full session fee.
- **AI Workflow Sprint ($2,500)**: At least 1 working AI workflow live and demonstrated before you leave, with your team trained to use it. If not achieved in the session, follow-up support included until it is.
- **AI Team Transformation ($4,500)**: Minimum 3 working AI workflows operational across your team within 60 days, with your team actively using them. If not, we return until they are — at no extra cost.

**Resource Requirements**
- Budget: <AUD $500 (website updates, Calendly, intake form tools — most free tier)
- Team: Shane only, ~20-25 hours total over 30 days (reduced from previous estimate; stealth removes LinkedIn content production time)
- Tools: Website CMS, Calendly (or cal.com free), Google Forms, Apollo.io or LinkedIn for outreach list

**Rough Economics (Side Project Context)**
- Investment: ~AUD $500 + 25 hours of Shane's time
- Expected revenue impact (conservative — side project capacity):
  - Pessimistic: 1 additional booking/month = AUD $2,500
  - Base: 2 bookings/month = AUD $7,500 (mix of tasters and sprints)
  - Optimistic: 3 workshops + 1 DFY upsell = AUD $20,000+
- Payback period: 1 booking (first workshop covers all investment)
- Break-even: 1 client

**Key Metrics**
- Leading indicators: outreach responses/week, referral partner inquiries, website product page views
- Lagging indicators: workshops booked/month at 90 days; DFY upsells at 90 days
- Kill criteria: 0 additional bookings in 60 days despite 4 weeks of active outreach AND 3 referral partner conversations → diagnose whether it's the offer, the targeting, or the capacity issue

## Scoring Key

| Dimension | Weight | Description |
|-----------|--------|-------------|
| Impact | ×3 | Revenue/growth potential |
| Feasibility | ×2 | Can you execute this with current resources and capabilities? |
| Speed | ×1 | Time to results |
| Risk | ×2 | Downside protection (5=safe, 1=catastrophic) |
| Optionality | ×2 | Opens future moves or locks you in? |
| Evidence | ×2 | Supported by current market data and research? |
| Strategic Fit | ×2 | Builds on existing capabilities, assets, relationships? |

**Scale**: 1–5 (1=very weak, 5=very strong)
