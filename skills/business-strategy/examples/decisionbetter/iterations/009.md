## New Option: Option M — AI-Delivered Training Platform

**What**: Build toward a Phase 3 state where DecisionBetter's training programs are substantially delivered by AI agents (built on Claude API + Claude Code + Anthropic Agent SDK), with Shane's involvement reduced to quality oversight and complex client escalations. The product: an AI agent that conducts workflow discovery, builds a custom training curriculum, delivers it conversationally, monitors implementation, and provides ongoing support — all tailored to the client's specific business, industry, and tools.

**Phase roadmap**:
- **Phase 1 (Now-12 months)**: Human-delivered, productized workshops. Shane builds and validates the curriculum IP with every workshop.
- **Phase 2 (12-24 months)**: Hybrid — AI handles pre/post-workshop (diagnostic agent, follow-up coaching, implementation monitoring). Shane handles facilitation only. Capacity doubles.
- **Phase 3 (24+ months)**: Substantially AI-delivered. Subscription model: $500-$1,500/month per company. Shane oversees quality, handles escalations, builds new modules.

**Why it might work**:
- The Anthropic stack (Claude API + Agent SDK) is exactly the right foundation for conversational training delivery
- Arist already demonstrates autonomous corporate training AI; the technology is proven in adjacent use cases
- DecisionBetter's core IP (workflow diagnosis, outcome measurement, AU SMB context) is the moat — not Shane's time
- Productized workshops = the curriculum architecture AI will deliver. Phase 1 IS Phase 3 preparation.
- The subscription model ($500-$1,500/month × 50-250 clients) creates a fundamentally different business than the workshop model ceiling

**Obvious risks**:
- Building a platform while delivering workshops is a split-attention problem
- AI training delivery may not replicate the trust-building Shane provides in person
- First-mover risk: Microsoft, Google building similar capabilities
- Timeline risk: 24+ months to Phase 3 is a long bet

**Tags**: Strategic bet (24+ months) | Segments: 1, 2, 3 (all segments, at lower price point than workshops)

## Key Insight from Iteration 9

The long-term vision (AI-delivered training) transforms the framing of all near-term decisions. Every productized workshop is not just a revenue event — it is curriculum validation for the AI delivery system. Every documented case study is training data. Every standard intake form is a prototype of the AI diagnostic agent. This means Option A is simultaneously a near-term revenue strategy AND the foundation of a 24-month platform build. The options are not separate bets; they are the same bet at different time horizons.

This is the most important structural insight of iterations 5-9: **DecisionBetter has a coherent three-phase strategy, not a collection of disconnected options**. The near-term moves (A, N, L, O, B) feed Phase 2 (J, D, G), which funds and validates Phase 3 (M). The stealth constraint actually helps here — it forces the business to build on relationships and demonstrated results rather than hype, which is exactly the foundation needed for a Phase 3 platform built on trust and proven methodology.
