## Research Hypothesis

I am stress-testing Option A (Productized Workshop Ladder) because it is the top-ranked option and its WWHTBT conditions have not yet been validated. Specifically:
1. Will AU SMB buyers transact on published pricing without a full discovery call?

## The Funnel View: Key Structural Insight from Iterations 0–4

The most important strategic insight emerging from the first five iterations is not any single option — it is the **funnel structure** that the options create together:

```
AWARENESS (Channels)
  ├── Industry Association Speaking (Option H) — reach skeptical SMB owners
  ├── Content-Led Inbound / LinkedIn (Option C) — compound authority building
  └── Platform Partner Directory (Option L) — passive technical credibility

ENTRY (First transaction)
  └── Budget AI Taster: $997, 3 hours, in-person (Option K)
      → "Is AI right for my business?"

CORE (Main offer)
  └── Productized Workshop Ladder (Option A):
      ├── AI Quick Win Sprint: $2,500 (half-day)
      ├── AI Team Immersion: $4,500 (full-day)
      └── AI Workflow Transformation: $12,000 (multi-session)

CONVERSION (High-value pipeline)
  └── Done For You (existing): $15K–$50K
  └── Industry Cohort (Option G): $4K × 8 participants = $32K per cohort

RETENTION (Recurring revenue — the structural fix)
  ├── AI Retainer Program (Option D): $1,500–$3,500/month
  └── Fractional AI Officer (Option J): $4,000–$8,000/month

REFERRAL (Scale without more time)
  └── Referral Partner Network (Option B): accountants/coaches refer Taster leads
```

This funnel is the business architecture DecisionBetter needs. Near-term priority: build the ENTRY and CORE layers (Options K + A). That pipeline feeds the CONVERSION and RETENTION layers, which solve the scaling problem identified in the diagnosis.

## Key Insight from This Iteration

**Productization works when it's outcome-led, not deliverable-led.** The difference between a commodity and a premium offer is not the price — it's the promise. "1-day workshop" is a commodity. "3 guaranteed workflow automations implemented and your team trained to maintain them, with 30-day support included" is a product. DecisionBetter's outcome guarantee is already the seed of this — it just needs to be the centre of the product name and description, not a footnote. This single framing shift is likely the highest-leverage change DecisionBetter can make to its existing offer, requiring no new capabilities and minimal website effort.
