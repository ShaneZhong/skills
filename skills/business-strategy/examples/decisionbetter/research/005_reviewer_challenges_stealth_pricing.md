## 2. AI Fatigue / Disillusionment Trough — Demand Implications

**Research hypothesis**: Does AI fatigue reduce or change the demand for consulting?

**Key finding — Gartner Trough framing (Informed opinion, consultancy.uk):**
- 2026 is in the trough of disillusionment on Gartner's hype cycle
- This is NOT a demand reduction — it is a demand SHIFT: from "AI exploration" to "AI ROI accountability"
- Companies that failed pilots are looking for implementers who can fix/improve, not evangelists selling possibilities

**Implication for DecisionBetter:**
- The Reviewer's concern about AI fatigue is valid but misframes the opportunity
- The new buyer psychology in 2026: "We tried AI, it didn't work, help us fix it" — this is actually **higher** willingness to pay than "AI is exciting"
- Positioning shift needed: from "AI as a coworker" (excitement-led) to "AI that actually works" (results-led)
- The outcome guarantee becomes MORE valuable in a trough, not less

**Source:** [Consultancy.uk AI Fatigue 2026](https://www.consultancy.uk/news/42610/consultants-point-to-ai-fatigue-and-organisational-overhauls-in-their-predictions-for-2026), [AI Disillusionment Benefits Finance Leaders](https://creativeplanning.com/insights/consulting/ai-trough-disillusionment-accounting-leaders/), [AI Didn't Kill Consulting](https://medium.com/@chestnut.david/ai-didnt-kill-consulting-it-might-just-save-its-soul-ab4052b4ef37)

## 4. Microsoft Copilot vs $997 Taster

**Research hypothesis**: Does Copilot Business ($18/user/month) commoditise the entry taster?

**Finding (Hard data — Pax8, VTC Tech):**
- Microsoft Copilot Business launched Dec 2025 at $18/user/month for SMBs <300 users
- Copilot Studio requires no programming background for basic agent creation
- This covers: email summarisation, meeting transcription, document drafting, basic workflow automation

**Distinction**:
- Copilot handles KNOWN workflows in Microsoft apps
- DecisionBetter's taster should identify UNKNOWN workflow opportunities, including non-Microsoft tools, and map the highest-ROI AI opportunities across the entire business (not just M365)
- The $997 taster's value prop must be repositioned: not "introduction to AI" but "which specific AI workflows will save YOUR business the most time and money — including things Copilot can't do"
- Target should be businesses already using Copilot who aren't getting value from it

## 5. Stealth-Compatible Acquisition Channels

**Research hypothesis**: What B2B acquisition channels work without any public social media presence?

**Key finding — Stealth B2B lead gen (Informed opinion):**
- Enterprise companies (Databricks) built $100M ARR before public launch via word-of-mouth and direct outreach
- Stealth-compatible channels: warm introductions, referral partnerships, private communities, direct outreach, strategic partnership co-selling, speaking at private/closed events (industry dinners, association member meetings — not public conferences)

**Specific channels that do NOT require public presence:**
1. **Direct cold outreach** (email + phone) — personalized, targeted, no public profile needed
2. **Referral partner networks** — accountants, business coaches, HR consultants referring clients
3. **Private/closed speaking** — industry association member dinners, chamber breakfast briefings (these are CLOSED events, not public conferences)
4. **Strategic co-selling** — partner with complementary service providers who refer AI work
5. **Warm network activation** — systematize what Shane has already been doing informally

**Key insight**: LinkedIn POSTS are the problem (public). LinkedIn DIRECT MESSAGES are stealth-compatible. The difference: no public content, but active 1:1 outreach.

**Source:** [Stealth Startup Strategies JPMorgan](https://www.jpmorgan.com/insights/banking/commercial-banking/stealth-startups-benefits-and-strategies-for-founders), [G2M Team Stealth Marketing](https://www.g2mteam.com/our-expertise/stealth-mode-marketing/)
