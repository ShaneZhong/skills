# Research: Iteration 008 — Stealth-Compatible Acquisition Channels
*Date: March 2026*

---

## Research Hypothesis

I am researching stealth-compatible B2B acquisition channels because the owner confirmed DecisionBetter is a stealth/side project with no public social media presence. I need to identify which acquisition channels work without LinkedIn posts or public marketing, and how to replace the previous content marketing assumption.

---

## The Stealth Constraint Defined

**What is NOT allowed**: Public social media posts (LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Instagram), public podcast guest appearances, public conference talks listed on event websites, press releases, public directory listings that expose the business name prominently.

**What IS allowed**: Everything that is private, relationship-driven, or passive (discovered by people who are already looking):
- 1:1 direct outreach (cold email, direct LinkedIn messages — not posts)
- Private/closed community participation (member-only groups, invite-only forums)
- Closed industry association member events (not public conferences)
- Referral partnerships (private referral arrangements with complementary service providers)
- Website with SEO content (passive discovery — people who search find it; no amplification)
- Anthropic partner directory listing (passive — listed as a resource, not a public broadcast)
- Warm network activation (personal conversations with existing contacts)

---

## Stealth-Compatible Channel Analysis

### Channel 1: Direct Outreach (Cold Email + LinkedIn DM)
**Mechanism**: Personalised, targeted outreach to specific business owners/operators in Shane's target segments. No public posts — 1:1 messages only.

**Why it works stealthily**:
- Completely private — no public profile needed
- Highest relevance when targeted by industry/role/company size
- AU cold email open rates (B2B): 40-47% for targeted campaigns (source: Growleady research, informed opinion)
- Response rates: 3-8% for personalised cold email; higher for warm intros

**What's required**:
- A list of target prospects (LinkedIn Sales Navigator, Apollo.io, or manual research)
- A personalised opening (reference specific industry challenges)
- A specific offer to mention (the productized workshop ladder)
- Shane's time: ~30 minutes/day for 10 personalised messages

**Volume estimates** (10 messages/day × 5 days/week × 8 weeks):
- 400 outreaches
- 3% response rate = 12 conversations
- 30% conversion to first booking = 3-4 first clients

**Stealth rating**: 10/10 — fully private, no public trace

---

### Channel 2: Referral Partner Network (Accountants, Business Coaches, HR Consultants)
**Mechanism**: Identify 10-15 complementary service providers who serve the same SMB client base. Establish informal or formal referral arrangements ($500-$1,500 finder's fee or reciprocal referrals).

**Why it works stealthily**:
- All referral conversations are private
- Partners don't mention DecisionBetter publicly — they mention Shane personally to clients
- No public profile needed; the partner's trust vouches for Shane

**AU-specific opportunity**:
- Accountants and bookkeepers actively refer technology and process improvement services to clients (confirmed: SMB Consultants AU partnership model — smbconsultants.com.au)
- Business coaches (Business Coach Institute AU, various ICF coaches) refer specialist implementation services when they identify technology gaps in client businesses
- HR consultants (for the Workshop Buyer segment) are actively looking for AI training providers for client teams

**What's required**:
- Shane's existing professional network to identify first 5 referral partners
- A simple 1-page "partner brief" explaining what DecisionBetter does, who to refer, and the referral arrangement
- Regular (monthly) check-ins with partners to stay top of mind

**Conversion characteristics**:
- Warm referrals from trusted advisors have 40-60% conversion rates (vs. 3-8% for cold outreach)
- Revenue per referral partner: 1 referral/month × 50% conversion × $4,500 avg = $2,250/month per active partner
- 5 active partners = $11,250/month additional revenue potential

**Stealth rating**: 10/10 — private referral arrangements; no public exposure

---

### Channel 3: Private Member Event Speaking (Closed Industry Events)
**Mechanism**: Speak at CLOSED events that are not publicly listed (private association dinners, member breakfast briefings, Chamber sub-committee meetings, peer CEO groups). These events:
- Have no public programme
- Are attended only by members
- Don't appear in Google search results
- Don't get written up in media

**Specific AU stealth-compatible venues**:
- CPA Australia member chapter events (closed to members only — not publicly listed)
- Australian Institute of Company Directors (AICD) chapter events
- Young Presidents' Organisation (YPO) chapter meetings
- Business Networking International (BNI) — private weekly meetings
- Sydney Chamber of Commerce member breakfasts (often unlisted until day-of)
- Industry-specific peer groups (e.g., TEC — The Executive Connection, Vistage-equivalent groups)

**What's required**:
- Identify 5-10 target groups where Shane has a contact or can request membership/guest speaker access
- Develop a 30-40 minute talk: "3 AI Workflows Every [Industry] Business Should Implement in 2026"
- CTA: specific product with booking link (not "visit my website")

**Volume estimates**:
- 1 speaking slot/month × 20 attendees × 10% follow-up rate × 40% conversion = 1 client/month from speaking
- Low volume, but high quality: attendees are pre-qualified (business owners/leaders) and trust the association

**Stealth rating**: 8/10 — the talk happens privately but Shane's name may appear in a members-only event notice. No public internet exposure.

---

### Channel 4: Private Online Communities (Slack/WhatsApp/Discord Groups)
**Mechanism**: Participate in invitation-only communities of AU business owners, operators, and decision-makers. Not posting content — providing genuine help and answers.

**Specific communities**:
- Slack communities for AU startups/scaleups (e.g., StartupAus private groups, Fishburners member Slack)
- LinkedIn Groups (semi-private — posts visible to group members, not general public feed)
- WhatsApp groups of business owner peers
- Industry-specific Discord servers (real estate, accounting, professional services)

**Why it works**:
- Position Shane as the "go-to person for AI questions" in private communities
- When a business owner asks "should I use Copilot or something else?" in a private Slack — Shane's answer leads to a conversation
- No public amplification; community members don't cross-post to their public feeds typically

**Volume**: Lower than direct outreach but higher-quality conversations. 1-2 inbound enquiries/month per active community.

**Stealth rating**: 8/10 — posts visible to community members only, but still a "public" audience within the community

---

### Channel 5: SEO Content (Website-Only, No Social Amplification)
**Mechanism**: Publish SEO-optimised blog content on the DecisionBetter website. Do NOT share on LinkedIn or social media. Allow organic Google discovery by people actively searching.

**Stealth-compatible approach**:
- Publish articles targeting long-tail queries: "AI workflow automation for accounting firms Sydney," "how to implement AI in professional services Australia"
- No social sharing — content is purely for Google discovery
- People who find this content are already in "research mode" — higher intent than social media audience

**Timeline**: SEO takes 6-12 months to generate meaningful traffic. Speed score is 1 for meaningful volume.

**Why still worth doing** (even as stealth):
- Passive and compounding — content written today generates leads indefinitely
- Establishes credibility for visitors who arrive via other channels (referrals, direct outreach responses)
- Supports the Anthropic partner directory listing (links to website as credibility)

**Volume at 12 months**: 100-300 organic visitors/month for a niche consultant site. At 2-3% conversion to inquiry: 2-9 inquiries/month.

**Stealth rating**: 9/10 — website exists publicly, but no amplification means minimal discovery until Google indexes the content

### Channel 6: Anthropic Claude Partner Directory (Passive Inbound)
**Mechanism**: Register in the Anthropic Claude Partner Network (launched March 12, 2026). Listed in the Services Partner Directory where enterprise and SMB buyers search for Claude implementation specialists.

**Stealth-compatible**: Yes — a directory listing is not a public broadcast. Shane's name/company appears to people actively searching for Anthropic implementation help. No LinkedIn announcement required.

**Strategic timing advantage**: The Claude Partner Network launched 5 days ago. Most AU SMB consultants have not registered yet. Early listing = first-mover advantage in the AU SMB segment of the directory.

**Volume**: Unknown (new directory). Comparable directories (Clutch, GoodFirms) generate a few inquiries/month for listed firms. Conservative estimate: 1-3 Anthropic-sourced inquiries/month within 6 months.

**Stealth rating**: 8/10 — listed publicly in the directory but only visible to people searching for Claude implementation partners

## Key Insight: Stealth Is Not a Handicap — It's a Filter

The stealth constraint eliminates the noisiest, most competitive acquisition channels (LinkedIn content) and forces investment in the highest-quality channels (referrals, direct relationships, targeted outreach). Referral-sourced clients convert at 40-60% vs. 3-8% for cold outreach, and 1-2% for content marketing. The stealth constraint inadvertently optimises the acquisition strategy.

Furthermore: in the AI consulting market of 2026, the content marketing channel is becoming saturated. Every AI consultant is posting "5 Ways AI Can Help Your Business" on LinkedIn. Differentiating through content is harder and slower. Differentiating through relationships and demonstrated results is a more defensible position.
