## Positioning

Arcadian Digital is primarily a **full-service digital agency** (web/mobile development, performance marketing) that has added an SMB AI consulting service module. Their SMB AI positioning: "AI isn't just for big companies — we make it simple and practical for small and medium businesses."

This is a classic agency "land and expand" model — they offer AI consulting as an add-on/entry point to a larger digital services relationship.

---

## Target Customer

- SMBs across Australia (primarily Melbourne-focused initially)
- Businesses already investing in digital/web presence
- Buyers who want a "one-stop shop" for digital + AI

---

## Services (SMB AI Module)

1. **1-Hour AI Business Discovery Session** — personalised AI consultation
   - Output: tailored AI roadmap, practical automation steps, no-jargon advice
2. AI for lead generation
3. AI for customer support
4. Marketing automation
5. Reporting automation
6. Workflow management

---

## Pricing

Discovery session pricing not publicly disclosed. Given they are an established agency, likely priced similarly to AIWise ($500–$1,500) or included as a sales tool for larger engagements. Implementation pricing undisclosed.

---

## Strengths

- Established brand (12 years in business) — credibility and trust
- Cross-sell opportunity: AI as entry point to broader digital services
- Melbourne location provides access to strong SMB market
- "No jargon" and accessible messaging (similar to DecisionBetter)
- Broad service offering reduces client need to go elsewhere

---

## Weaknesses

- AI is not their core competency — digital agency first, AI second
- No specialist AI depth (team may have broader digital skills, not deep AI/ML)
- No hands-on workshop product visible
- No outcome guarantee
- Melbourne-based — geographic competition with DecisionBetter is indirect (Sydney vs Melbourne)
- Larger agency structure may mean less personal service than solo/boutique providers

---

## Recent Moves

No AI-specific announcements found. The SMB AI consulting service page appears to be a recent addition to their established service portfolio (likely added 2024–2025 as AI became mainstream demand).

---

## Competitive Threat to DecisionBetter

**Low-Medium**. Arcadian Digital competes on geographic overlap only if DecisionBetter expands to Melbourne. More importantly, their model (agency add-on) differs from DecisionBetter's specialist positioning. However, they represent the risk of large incumbent agencies using AI as a "free add-on" to undercut specialist consultants.

**Key insight**: The proliferation of agencies adding AI modules (without deep expertise) actually strengthens the case for a specialist like DecisionBetter who can credibly claim deeper, more personalised AI knowledge.
