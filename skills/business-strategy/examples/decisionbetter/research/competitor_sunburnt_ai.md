## Positioning

Sunburnt AI positions as "Your Partners in Practical AI Strategy" — an emphasis on practical, outcome-oriented, locally-owned AI solutions. Their core messages:
- High-value, local, fast, and secure AI solutions
- "Data sovereignty, compliance, and performance" (an Australian-specific angle targeting data-conscious SMBs)
- AI strategy → identify where AI creates greatest impact → prioritise opportunities

Notably, they emphasise **Australian data sovereignty** — hosting/processing data locally rather than through US/global cloud providers. This could resonate with healthcare, legal, and finance SMBs.

---

## Target Customer

- SMBs and mid-market Australian businesses
- Industry-agnostic in messaging but likely stronger in regulated industries (healthcare, finance, legal) given sovereignty emphasis
- Buyers who want AI strategy before implementation

---

## Services

1. AI strategy and roadmapping
2. Secure, scalable cloud infrastructure for enterprise-grade AI
3. Advanced LLM integrations
4. Process automation
5. Implementation

**Philosophy**: Work begins with "clarity" — identifying where AI creates greatest impact before building anything. This is similar to DecisionBetter's diagnostic approach.

## Pricing

Not publicly disclosed. Discovery call model (same as DecisionBetter). Likely custom-quoted engagements.

## Strengths

- "Australian-owned and operated" data sovereignty angle — strong differentiator for regulated sectors
- Practical strategy-first positioning (avoids over-promising)
- Three-person founding team suggests broader capability than solo operators
- Security and compliance focus is increasingly important

---

## Weaknesses

- No visible workshop/training product
- No published pricing or social proof
- No clear SMB-specific go-to-market vs. general Australian business focus
- Data sovereignty angle may not resonate with all SMBs (more valuable for enterprise/regulated)

---

## Recent Moves

No public announcements found. New entrant — website appears to be early-stage marketing. No case studies or testimonials found in public search results.

---

## Competitive Threat to DecisionBetter

**Medium**. Sunburnt AI occupies a similar "practical, locally-focused AI strategy" position but targets a slightly different buyer (security/compliance-conscious vs. general SMB). If Sunburnt AI develops a workshop product, they would be a closer direct competitor.

**Key distinction**: DecisionBetter's hands-on workshop with real client data and outcome guarantee is not replicated by Sunburnt AI's model. Sunburnt AI appears more focused on infrastructure and strategy advisory.
