---

## Positioning

Wilson AI presents as an end-to-end AI/ML consulting firm for a broad range of industries. Their positioning spans:
- AI algorithms and custom model development
- Business process improvement through AI
- Wide industry coverage: health, retail, insurance, financial services, real estate, manufacturing, e-commerce

They market specifically to Australian businesses with a dedicated /AI-Consultants-Australia.php page, suggesting direct SEO competition with DecisionBetter's target audience.

---

## Target Customer

- Not specifically SMB-focused — appears to target mid-market up to enterprise
- Industry-agnostic positioning (breadth over depth)
- Geographic: Victoria/national

---

## Services

Based on website and available information:
- AI strategy consulting
- Custom AI/ML development
- AI implementation
- Business transformation

**Gaps vs. DecisionBetter**:
- No visible "hands-on workshop" or training product
- No "outcome guarantee" messaging
- No "non-technical" positioning — appears to target more technically-aware buyers
- No explicit SMB pricing

---

## Pricing

Not publicly disclosed. Likely custom-quoted. Given ~$362K revenue estimate and boutique size, probably day-rate or project-fee model in the AUD $5K–$50K range per engagement.

---

## Strengths

- Established since 2018 — 7+ years in market (trust signal)
- SEO presence for "AI Consultants Australia" search terms
- Broad industry expertise allows cross-sector referrals
- Technical depth (ML/AI algorithm focus)

---

## Weaknesses

- Small revenue base suggests limited scale or market penetration
- Broad positioning may mean no deep niche ownership
- No visible workshop/training product to acquire leads at lower commitment level
- Not specifically SMB-friendly in messaging
- No public social proof (reviews, case studies visible in research)

---

## Recent Moves

No public announcements found (as of March 2026). No funding, acquisitions, or major partnerships identified.

---

## Competitive Threat to DecisionBetter

**Medium**. Wilson AI competes for the same "AI consulting Australia" search terms and may bid on similar SMB projects. However, DecisionBetter's hands-on non-technical workshop approach and outcome guarantee create meaningful differentiation. Wilson AI's technical focus means they're less likely to win deals where the buyer's primary concern is "my team actually needs to learn to use this."
